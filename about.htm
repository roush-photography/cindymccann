Cindy McCann started her career as a tax preparation specialist in the early 1990's, working for a national tax service. Her diligence, honestly, and straight-forward thinking quickly brought her a solid base of loyal customers. Her knowledge of the tax laws and finances also helped her retain these very customers over the years, as each returned for advice and filings every year since.

Cindy is a certified RTRP (Registered Tax Return Preparer), a certificate earned by only those who can pass the rigorous testing process. She is also an Enrolled Agent, meaning she can represent you in any tax court in the United States should you be facing an audit.

Her list of tax services is a long one, but included are: Inheritance Tax 706 / Quarterly Payments 941 & 940 / 1099 & W2 Services for Corporations, Partnerships, and Sole Proprietors. She is also versed in assisting with Tax Liens & Offers in Compromise.

Having a solid financial partner in your personal life and in your business is not only important these days, but it is also a huge comfort; knowing you are doing things right on all of the government issued paperwork and taxes.

Currently Cindy has moved on to running her own Tax Service Business in Humboldt Tennessee. If you contact her now you can become one of the many who enjoy the confidence of her services.
